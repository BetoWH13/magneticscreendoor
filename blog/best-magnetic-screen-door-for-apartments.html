Best Magnetic Screen Door for Apartments

If you rent and want to enjoy fresh air without letting in bugs, a magnetic screen door is one of the easiest upgrades you can make. Here's what makes one apartment-friendly—and which options are best.

What to Look for in a Rental-Friendly Screen

  • No tools required: Look for full-frame Velcro installation, no screws or nails.
  • Standard sizing: Most apartment doors are 36" x 80"—stick to that unless you measured differently.
  • Easy to remove: Adhesive should come off clean without damaging paint or trim.

Best All-Around Pick

Thousands of renters have gone with the Flux Phenom magnetic screen door on Amazon. It’s easy to install, renter-safe, and sized to fit most standard apartment doors.

Quick Install Tips

  • Clean the door frame with rubbing alcohol before applying Velcro
  • Use a level if you want a perfect hang
  • Don’t stretch the mesh too tight—it should hang naturally

With the right fit and a clean install, you’ll keep bugs out all summer without ticking off your landlord.
