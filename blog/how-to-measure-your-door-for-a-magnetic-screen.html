How to Measure Your Door for a Magnetic Screen

Before buying a magnetic screen door, make sure it’ll actually fit your doorway. Here's a quick guide that skips the fluff and gets you the numbers you need.

1. Measure the Door Opening

Use a tape measure to get the width and height of the actual door opening—not the trim or frame.

  • Width: Measure across the top of the inside opening.
  • Height: Measure from the top edge to the floor or threshold.

2. Compare to Standard Sizes

Most magnetic screens are made for common sizes like:

  • 36" x 80" (standard door)
  • 38" x 82" (taller doors)
  • Up to 72" wide (for French or double doors)

If your measurements are close, pick a screen with a little extra overlap. Velcro edges will help you seal it snugly.

3. Don’t Forget Clearance

Check if you have enough flat surface around the door frame to stick the Velcro. Some tight frames or textured surfaces may need extra prep.
